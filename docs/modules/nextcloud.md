Set up your own personal cloud for files, calendar, contacts, and more.

---

## What is Nextcloud?

Nextcloud is a self-hosted alternative to Google Drive, Dropbox, and iCloud. You control your data.

### Features
- File sync across devices
- Calendar and contacts
- Photo backup
- Document editing
- Video calls
